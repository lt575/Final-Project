PlanPal: Frequently Asked Questions
Main Page

The Website itself

Q: What is the purpose of this Website

A: This website is meant to help you plan your events and perhaps interact with other users.

Q: What kind of events or planning can I do?

A: Any kind, you can plan events like birthdays, or try and build a habit tracker.

Scheduling features

Q: Why can't I schedule a time with someone?

A: Check to see if the time you want isn't already filled.

Q: I still can't schedule time with someone even if their time slot is available?

A: It could be that you are not clicking the book event button, if not that, then it could be your computer having an issue.

Customization

Q: Am I able to personalize/customize my profile?

A: Yes, you should be able to set your page to your theme preferences with a theme selector in options.
